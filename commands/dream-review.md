# Ultraprompt Dream Review

Inspect recent dream reports, summaries, emitted candidates, failures, and next governance actions.

## Usage

- `/ultraprompt:dream-review`
- `/ultraprompt:dream-review --limit 25`

## Dispatch

Invoke `dream_review` and summarize report IDs, job names, candidates, failures, and recommended approvals or rejections.
